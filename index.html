About

I am a Data Scientist at Sidewalk Labs in New York City with a PhD in Particle Physics from New York University (NYU) where I worked with Kyle Cranmer on physics analyses at ATLAS and the statistical analysis tool RooStats which is part of CERN’s Root Data Analysis tool. My ATLAS work at CERN was focused on the Higgs Boson discovery, the first mass measurement, the first evidence for VBF production and property measurements related to the coupling strengths.
